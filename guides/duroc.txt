:large_orange_diamond: **Things to Note**
        :small_red_triangle_down: Duroc uses Guard after fixed point of his Hp
        :small_red_triangle_down: His attacks become stronger after each Guard
        :small_red_triangle_down: Tank is stunned in every cycle
        :small_red_triangle_down: Duroc A must not be hit with Physical Damage
        :small_red_triangle_down: Duroc B must not be hit with Magical Damage
